Privacy Policy

How Event Sphere handles personal information.

This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and the choices available to customers, organizers, and visitors.

1. Information we collect

We may collect account details, contact information, order history, event attendance records, organizer profile information, support messages, device data, and usage activity. Payment details may be processed by payment providers and are not stored in full by Event Sphere.

2. How we use information

  • Provide event discovery, ticket checkout, QR tickets, and account features.
  • Process orders, refunds, support requests, and organizer workflows.
  • Protect customers, organizers, and the platform from fraud or abuse.
  • Send transactional messages and optional marketing updates.
  • Improve reliability, usability, reporting, and marketplace quality.

3. Sharing information

We may share information with event organizers, payment processors, service providers, support tools, analytics providers, legal authorities when required, and other parties involved in completing the transaction or operating the event.

4. Cookie Policy

Event Sphere may use cookies and similar technologies to keep users signed in, remember preferences, measure site performance, improve security, and understand how visitors use the marketplace. You can manage browser cookie settings, but some features may not work correctly without essential cookies.

5. Marketing choices

You may opt out of promotional emails by using unsubscribe controls when available or by contacting support. Transactional messages about purchases, account security, event changes, and support requests may still be sent.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the platform, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, support financial records, and maintain marketplace integrity.

7. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information. No internet service is completely secure, so users should keep passwords private and report suspicious account activity promptly.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or export certain personal information. Contact support@eventsphere.com to submit a privacy request. We may need to verify your identity before acting on the request.

9. Children

Event Sphere is not intended for children who are too young to consent to online services under applicable law. Event organizers may also set age restrictions for specific events.

10. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to support@eventsphere.com. Please include "Privacy Request" in the subject line for faster routing.
